Investment Progression

F.R.I.E.N.D. begins with a modest Pre-Pilot foundation, then expands only after the concept is validated through store-level use, measured outcomes, and leadership approval at each gate.

Funding principle: Each phase should be validated before advancing to the next stage. This protects the organization, reduces implementation risk, and ensures leadership is investing against measurable progress.

Executive takeaway: The $8.1M investment is a phased strategy to build operational intelligence, support leaders, guide associates, and create a scalable division-wide execution system. It is not a one-time software purchase; it is an investment in stronger stores, stronger people, and measurable results.
